Oven cooked bacon

Nice crispy bacon.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound thick cut bacon

Directions:

  1. Heat oven to 400 degrees.

  2. Line a large baking sheet with aluminum foil.

  3. Arrange bacon strips on the baking sheet. Place into oven.

  4. Cook bacon for 14 minutes or until desired.

  5. Move bacon to paper towel lined plates to soak excess grease.
