Scrambled Eggs

Easy scrambled eggs.

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon butter
  • 1 teaspoon water (Optional)
  • 1 pinch salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. In a cup or small bowl, whisk together the eggs and water.

  2. Melt butter in a skillet over low heat.

  3. Pour in eggs and stir continuously as they cook.

  4. Remove the eggs when they are set but still moist.

  5. Plate and add salt and pepper.
